About the Program

Ethiopia's first structured startup-to-SOE innovation bridge

Backed by Ethiopian Investment Holdings — the government body behind Ethiopia's most powerful enterprises.

JumpStart Ethiopia is a Corporate Open Innovation and Venture Building program that links high-potential startups with State-Owned Enterprises through strategic partnership, commercial collaboration, and targeted investment.

SOEs gain access to startup agility and fresh thinking. Founders gain real customers, infrastructure, mentorship, and pathways to investment — without giving away equity at the door.

For Founders

Real SOE customers, grants, cloud credits, legal advisory, and investment pathways.

For SOEs

Solve operational bottlenecks faster than traditional R&D. De-risked innovation through paid pilots.

For Investors

Access startups validated through real SOE demand — de-risked deal flow at every stage.

For Ethiopia

A coordinated ecosystem where roles are clear and resources compound rather than fragment.
